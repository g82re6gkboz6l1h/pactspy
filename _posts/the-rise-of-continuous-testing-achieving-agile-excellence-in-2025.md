## Why Continuous Testing Matters in 2025

Continuous testing executes automated tests throughout the SDLC, ensuring quality at every stage. With the complexities of modern software, accelerated release cycles, and growing customer expectations, continuous testing is no longer optional.

### Key Advantages:

- **Tackle Complexity:** Modern applications span across cloud, IoT, edge computing, and mobile platforms.
- **Speedier Releases:** Minimize delays by detecting defects early.
- **Meet Customer Expectations:** Deliver flawless user experiences.
- **Adapt to Fast Tech Changes:** Stay ahead despite rapid technological shifts.
