## Why Security Testing Needs to Evolve With Rising Cyber Threats

The rapid digital transformation of businesses has expanded attack surfaces exponentially. With vulnerabilities spanning cloud systems, IoT devices, APIs, and mobile apps, organizations now face escalating cyber risks. Cybercrime damages are projected to exceed trillions annually, forcing enterprises to revisit their security frameworks.

Traditional methodologies like manual penetration testing and static vulnerability assessments lack agility and scalability to counter dynamic, evolving threats such as zero-day vulnerabilities. AI-based security testing, a transformative innovation, addresses these shortcomings by automating tasks, detecting patterns, and providing predictive insights. Organizations equipped with such tools gain a substantial edge over tech-savvy adversaries.
