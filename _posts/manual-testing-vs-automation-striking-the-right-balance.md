### Manual vs Automation: A Comprehensive Comparison

- **Manual Testing** focuses on creativity, intuition, and uncovering edge cases. It excels in exploratory tests, UI evaluations, and dynamically changing projects but can be time-consuming.
- **Automation Testing** leverages tools for repetitive tasks and large datasets, offering speed, precision, and reusability but requires upfront investment and lacks creativity.
