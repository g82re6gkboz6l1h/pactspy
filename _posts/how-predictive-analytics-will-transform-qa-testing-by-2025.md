## What Is Predictive Analytics in QA Testing?

Predictive analytics leverages data, statistical methods, and artificial intelligence to forecast potential problems before they occur. Within QA testing, these techniques proactively detect bugs, glitches, performance issues, and errors, making testing faster and smarter.

Traditional QA testing is reactive—addressing problems after they've arisen. Predictive analytics flips the model by predicting issues based on historical and real-time data. This ensures teams allocate resources effectively and mitigate risks before software hits the market.

### Techniques Revolutionizing QA Testing:
- **Regression Analysis:** Forecasts defect probability using prior results.
- **Classification Models:** Identifies high-risk modules for focused testing.
- **Anomaly Detection:** Flags deviations to prevent critical failures in real-time.
