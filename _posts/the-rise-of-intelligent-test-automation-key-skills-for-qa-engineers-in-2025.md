## Intelligent Test Automation Explained

ITA integrates AI and ML into automated testing processes to achieve higher efficiency, predictive capabilities, and continuous adaptability. Unlike traditional automation tools, ITA empowers systems to learn, identify issues proactively, and adjust test cases dynamically.

### Core Advantages of ITA:
- **Predictive Analysis**: Pinpoint vulnerabilities using historical data trends.
- **Adaptive Testing**: Automate test case revisions based on evolving codebases.
- **Self-Healing Scripts**: Minimize human intervention by repairing broken scripts automatically.
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Detect edge cases and gaps often missed during manual processes.
