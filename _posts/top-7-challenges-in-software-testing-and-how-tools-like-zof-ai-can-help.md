## 1. Traditional QA Challenges & Limitations

Software testing has long been a resource-intensive process reliant on manual effort. QA teams often struggle with:

- Balancing manual vs. automated testing.
- Managing time within rapid development cycles.
- Maximizing test coverage across devices and platforms.
- Budget concerns for maintaining high-quality releases.

Traditional automation tools only partially solve these challenges, often lacking adaptability and scalability. AI advancements are transforming the limits of what’s possible in quality assurance.
