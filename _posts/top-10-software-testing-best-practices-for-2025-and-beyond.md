3. Harnessing Shift-Left and Shift-Right Testing Approaches

- **Shift-left testing:** Catch bugs early by integrating QA at the design and development stages.
- **Shift-right testing:** Test software in live environments to address real-world performance challenges.

Combining these strategies allows teams to create secure, user-centric applications faster.

4. Strengthening Security and Compliance

Security testing will be a top priority by 2025. Best practices include:
- Regular penetration tests
- Automated vulnerability scans via tools like Zof AI
- Real-world privacy-first testing

Ensure GDPR, HIPAA, or global compliance standards are met for maximum safety and legal conformity.
