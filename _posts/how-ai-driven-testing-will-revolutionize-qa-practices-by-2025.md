Smart Testing Strategies with AI

AI has transformed QA from reactive problem-solving to proactive, insight-driven strategies. Common AI-driven methods include:

1. **Test Prioritization:** Focuses on high-risk cases by analyzing historical patterns.
2. **Automated Regression Testing:** Ensures stable updates by detecting bugs after code changes.
3. **User Simulation:** Mimics real customer behaviors to optimize test cases and detect flaws.
4. **CI/CD Integration:** Introduces AI into Continuous Integration/Delivery pipelines for real-time feedback.

These strategies reduce manual intervention, improve testing accuracy, and allow QA teams to spend more time on innovation.
