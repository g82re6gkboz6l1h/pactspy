Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing software testing by enabling predictive, dynamic, and efficient automation processes. By 2025, expect these advancements to dominate quality assurance practices:

2. **Dynamic Test Adaptability**: Real-time adjustments in test case generation aligned with application updates.
3. **Rapid Bug Diagnostics**: AI-enabled actionable insights to identify and fix issues faster.
