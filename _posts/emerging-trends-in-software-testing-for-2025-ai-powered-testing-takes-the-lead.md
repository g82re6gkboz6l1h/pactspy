## The Transformative Role of AI and ML in Test Automation

AI and ML are revolutionizing the traditional test automation landscape by using intelligent algorithms to streamline and enhance testing efficiency. Unlike older approaches, AI-driven automation adapts dynamically to changes in requirements and application environments.

### Key Innovations in AI and ML for Testing

1. **Test Case Automation and Prioritization**  
   AI generates and prioritizes test cases based on historical data and system usage patterns, ensuring full coverage of critical areas.

2. **Defect Prediction and Mitigation**  
   Machine learning algorithms identify coding patterns prone to errors and detect them early, reducing costs and development time.

3. **Self-Healing Scripts**  
   AI tools repair broken test scripts caused by app changes, minimizing manual updates for faster processes.

4. **Enhanced Performance Testing**  
   AI predicts software behavior under simulated stress, spotting bottlenecks with minimal manual effort.
