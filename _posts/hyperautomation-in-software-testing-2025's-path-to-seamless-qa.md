## What is Hyperautomation in Software Testing?

Hyperautomation refers to an advanced automation process that leverages technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to automate extensive workflows. Specifically, in software testing, it makes QA smarter, adaptive, and end-to-end automated while reducing human intervention.

Unlike traditional automated testing methods that rely on hard-coded scripts, hyperautomation dynamically adjusts, learns over time, and orchestrates QA processes without direct input. The result? Higher speed, reliability, and broader test coverage.
