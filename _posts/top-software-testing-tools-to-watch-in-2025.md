Manual vs. Automated Testing

Choosing between manual and automated testing depends on project goals. Here's a breakdown of their differences:

### Manual Testing

**Benefits:**
- Enables exploratory testing and creative problem-solving.
- Useful for early-stage feature testing without predefined test cases.

**Challenges:**
- Time-intensive and resource-heavy.
- Hard to scale, especially for complex projects.

### Automated Testing

**Benefits:**
- Consistent, repeatable results.
- Ideal for regression and performance testing.
- Scalable and reduces human resource dependency.

**Challenges:**
- Requires upfront investment in scripts and tools.
- Limited capacity for exploratory testing.
