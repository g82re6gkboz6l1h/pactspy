Shift-Left and Shift-Right Testing Approaches

The integration of shift-left and shift-right methodologies will define the future of testing strategies by:

- **Shift-Left:** Focusing on bug prevention via early testing alongside development.
- **Shift-Right:** Enhancing post-deployment via real-time monitoring, A/B testing, and chaos engineering.

Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) unify these practices, streamlining workflows and providing actionable insights across the software lifecycle.
