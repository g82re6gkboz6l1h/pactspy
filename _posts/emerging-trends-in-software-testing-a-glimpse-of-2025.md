Codeless Frameworks Revolutionizing QA

Codeless testing frameworks are eliminating technical barriers, making automated testing accessible to all team members—beyond developers. Powered by AI, these frameworks:

- Simplify test creation through drag-and-drop interfaces
- Adapt to software changes with self-maintaining mechanisms
- Integrate with CI/CD pipelines for continuous tracking

**Why This Matters:** By adopting codeless solutions, businesses can scale testing without needing extensive technical expertise, saving time and resources.
