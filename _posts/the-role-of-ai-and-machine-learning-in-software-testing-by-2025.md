## Unlocking the Future of Software Testing: AI and Machine Learning's Influence by 2025

Software testing is evolving rapidly as AI and machine learning (ML) technologies redefine the field. Traditional manual testing can no longer keep pace with increasingly complex applications and fast development cycles. As we approach 2025, AI is proving indispensable, enabling smarter, more accurate testing workflows and transforming quality assurance (QA) practices.
