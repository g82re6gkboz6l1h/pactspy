By the 2020s, automated testing capabilities had become more advanced, featuring intelligent frameworks with data-driven analytics and workflow automation.

As 2025 looms closer, AI is revolutionizing QA by enabling self-learning and automation systems. Tools like Zof AI now employ machine learning to predict vulnerabilities, identify bugs, dynamically adapt test cases, and improve testing accuracy.
