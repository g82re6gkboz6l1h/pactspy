Continuous testing is emerging as the gold standard for software QA processes, replacing outdated practices that occurred post-development. By embedding testing throughout the development lifecycle, organizations reduce feedback cycles, speed up delivery, and enhance end-to-end quality.

As AR, VR, and quantum computing technologies gain momentum, software testing faces unparalleled challenges. Here’s a look at the key hurdles and strategies for addressing them:

1. **AR/VR Testing**: AR/VR software requires meticulous testing to assess immersion quality, motion tracking, rendering performance, and latency. Teams must simulate real-world scenarios across platforms to ensure flawless user experiences.

2. **Quantum Computing Testing**: As quantum computing evolves, testers will need specialized expertise to evaluate algorithms’ behavior, addressing nuances in quantum states like superpositions and entanglement.
