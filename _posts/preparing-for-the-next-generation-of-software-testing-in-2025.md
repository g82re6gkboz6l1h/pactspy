Emerging Technologies Reshaping Software Testing

### Blockchain Testing
Blockchain applications now include supply chain management, secure voting, and financial transactions. Rigorous testing of encryption, smart contracts, and system integrations is vital to prevent irreversible errors.

### IoT Testing
Billions of IoT devices like smart homes, autonomous vehicles, and industrial sensors create extensive networks. Testing must focus on device interoperability, scalability under high loads, and security of data communication.

### AI Testing
AI systems bring unique testing challenges with dynamic learning models. Testing involves validating output accuracy, minimizing biases, and ensuring scalability. Platforms like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) streamline AI system validation for consistent and ethical performance.
