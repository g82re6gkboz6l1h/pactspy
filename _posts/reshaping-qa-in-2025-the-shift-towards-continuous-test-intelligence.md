## What is Continuous Test Intelligence?

Continuous test intelligence is a step beyond mere automation. It integrates advanced AI systems and dynamic feedback mechanisms into the software lifecycle. By analyzing real-time data, QA teams gain actionable insights throughout the development process—ensuring testing is fully embedded rather than compartmentalized as a final step.

### Core Principles of Continuous Test Intelligence:
1. **AI-Driven Testing**: Incorporates AI tools to automate intelligently and detect anomalies early.
2. **Iterative Feedback**: Continuous real-time feedback ensures minimal delays and faster problem resolution.
3. **Proactive Monitoring**: Live insights and predictive analytics provide deeper test coverage and preempt bottlenecks.

In this evolving landscape, testing becomes a real-time, continuous effort rather than a periodic activity—enabling innovation at speed.
