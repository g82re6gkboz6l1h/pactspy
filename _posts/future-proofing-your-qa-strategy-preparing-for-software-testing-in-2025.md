Continuous Testing for Agile Delivery

Continuous delivery (CD) and continuous testing (CT) are game-changers in modern software development. These models embed testing processes within the development lifecycle, allowing seamless iterations and eliminating traditional silos.

### Advantages of Continuous Delivery and Testing:
1. **Quick Feedback Loops**: Gather real-time insights from automated testing.
2. **Reduced Risks**: Catch bugs early and avoid costly post-deployment fixes.
3. **Accelerated Delivery**: Ship updates faster while maintaining quality.

### Implementation Tips:
- Foster collaboration between QA, DevOps, and development teams.
- Automate testing pipelines using advanced tools like Zof AI.
- Integrate QA within CI/CD workflows to improve efficiency and agility.
