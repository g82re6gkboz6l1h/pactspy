### Emerging Testing Trends for 2025

Modern systems demand dynamic testing approaches capable of tackling complexity. Industry-leading methodologies include:

#### **1. Risk-Based Testing**
Efficiently allocates resources to high-impact areas, prioritizing critical application zones prone to failure.

#### **2. Model-Based Testing**
Employs abstract models to simulate system behaviors, automatically generating scalable and accurate test cases.

#### **3. Chaos Engineering for Resiliency**
Designed to test distributed systems under stress, chaos engineering validates robustness during unexpected events.

#### **4. Mutation Testing**
Strengthens test reliability through intentional code changes, ensuring robust error detection.

2. **Security Testing**: Encryption, data protection, and compliance validation are essential for safeguarding IoT networks.
3. **Interoperability**: Hybrid ecosystems involving mobile, cloud, and edge servers pose challenges in seamless functionality.
