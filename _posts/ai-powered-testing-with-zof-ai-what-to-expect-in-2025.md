AR/VR platforms, conversational AI, and voice-powered apps will become a primary focus of AI testing tools, leveraging capabilities like computer vision and NLP.

### 6. **Ethical AI Testing Standards**
Zof AI will champion responsible AI by embedding unbiased, secure practices, ensuring even self-regulating mechanisms are robustly tested.
