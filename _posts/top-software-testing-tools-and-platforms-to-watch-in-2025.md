- **Integration:** Does the tool work with Git, CI/CD pipelines, and project management platforms?

- **AI & Automation:** Is the tool prepared for modern AI-driven workflows?
- **Security Features:** Does it effectively identify vulnerabilities?

Successful QA teams in 2025 will embrace hybrid approaches that combine **low-code/no-code tools** with robust, highly configurable platforms like **Zof AI**.
