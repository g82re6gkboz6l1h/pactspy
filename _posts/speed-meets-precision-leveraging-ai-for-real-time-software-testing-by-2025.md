## What is Real-Time Testing?
Real-time testing integrates QA into every stage of development, catching bugs dynamically as they arise instead of after completion. Powered by AI, this approach ensures faster defect detection, better collaboration, and enhanced software reliability—an evolution that eliminates traditional bottlenecks and improves development speeds.
