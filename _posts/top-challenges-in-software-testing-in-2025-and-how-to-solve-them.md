Challenge #4: Safeguarding Data During Testing

With data breaches on the rise, securing sensitive information during testing is paramount, requiring focus on exposure risks, unsecure environments, and inadvertent leaks.

### Solutions
- **Data masking:** Anonymize data to minimize exposure risks without compromising results.
- **Cloud security:** Enforce encrypted and access-controlled protocols in testing environments.
- **Compliance audits:** Regularly ensure adherence to GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy standards.
