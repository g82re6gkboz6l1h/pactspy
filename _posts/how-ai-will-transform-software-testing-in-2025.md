Among the most promising applications of AI is defect detection — an indispensable aspect of QA. AI technology leverages machine learning to detect bugs and identify patterns in historical data, system telemetry, and user behavior.

### Automated Bug Detection
Platforms like Zof AI identify bugs in real time, cross-referencing test failures against known defect patterns. The system automatically pinpoints root causes, accelerating debugging processes.

### Maximized Test Coverage

### Dynamic Test Case Updates
