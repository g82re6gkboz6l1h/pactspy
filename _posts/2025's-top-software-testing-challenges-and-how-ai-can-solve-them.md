## Software Testing Challenges in 2025

1. **Rapidly Increasing Software Complexity**: Sophisticated algorithms and multi-layered architectures demand extensive validation in performance, security, and usability.
2. **Testing AI-Powered Applications**: Assessing bias, edge behaviors, and dynamic learning exposes limitations of traditional approaches.
3. **IoT Expanded Networks**: Testing seamless communication among interconnected IoT devices is labor-intensive.
4. **Faster Iteration Demands**: Agile and DevOps workflows prioritize speed over extended testing durations.
5. **Diverse Data**: Personalization and global user bases create unforeseen edge-case challenges.
