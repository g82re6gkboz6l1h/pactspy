## Understanding the Role of AI in Modern QA Practices

Traditional software testing is repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to error. AI optimizes these processes, offering capabilities such as test case generation, error prediction, and test optimization:

- **Test Optimization**: AI analyzes vulnerabilities and prioritizes high-risk areas.
- **Test Case Generation**: AI can automatically generate tests with comprehensive coverage.
- **Error Prediction**: With predictive analytics, QA teams can tackle bugs early.
