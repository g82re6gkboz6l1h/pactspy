## Evolution of Automated Testing

Automated testing started with basic scripting but matured with frameworks like Selenium, Appium, and JUnit. However, challenges such as frequent script maintenance and lack of adaptability persist. AI now paves the way for smarter, dynamic, and self-healing test solutions, minimizing manual intervention.

### 7. **User Experience Testing**
