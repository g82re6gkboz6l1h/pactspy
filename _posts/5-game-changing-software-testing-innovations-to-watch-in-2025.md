3. Blockchain: Elevating Security within Testing Frameworks

Blockchain technology, with its immutable ledger system, is revolutionizing testing processes in security-intensive industries like healthcare and finance. Key benefits include:

- **Immutable logs** for comprehensive accountability.
- **Tamper-proof testing environments** ensuring data integrity.
- Improved **IoT security testing** by safeguarding device communication.

Expect blockchain adoption for secure testing to become standard by 2025.
