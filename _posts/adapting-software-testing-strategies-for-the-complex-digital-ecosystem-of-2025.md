## Why Testing Strategies Must Evolve

Traditional testing methods fall short in addressing the complexities of:

1. **IoT Integration** with interconnected devices and protocols.
2. **AI Decision-Making** requiring testing of deterministic and non-deterministic behaviors.
3. **Blockchain Security and Scalability** in decentralized systems.
4. **Performance Expectations** for always-on, real-time systems.
