## Why QA Faces Unique Challenges in 2025

The emergence of IoT, blockchain-powered apps, and microservices architectures are complicating QA efforts. Additionally, Agile and DevOps methodologies continue to shrink testing windows, making QA adaptation critical.

#### Rapid Changes That QA Teams Must Address:
- **Product Complexity:** IoT devices and other hi-tech systems involve intricate workflows that demand exhaustive QA processes.
- **Cloud-Native Testing:** Applications need validation across AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and hybrid deployments.
- **Scaling Techniques:** Microservices architectures create dependencies that traditional tools struggle to match.
