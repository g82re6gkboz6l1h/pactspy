## The Role of AI in Modern QA Processes

Historically, QA relied on manual testing or traditional automation tools, both of which require extensive script creation and ongoing maintenance. The advent of AI, however, introduces game-changing advantages, enabling testing processes to:

- Predict vulnerabilities and potential failures using machine learning (ML) algorithms.
- Self-adjust testing scripts to adapt to UI or application logic changes.
- Conduct unsupervised exploratory testing.
- Create precise test cases from historical data and analytics for smarter testing strategies.

By leveraging AI’s learning capabilities, QA teams can expect higher precision, reduced testing times, and faster identification of defects before software goes live.
