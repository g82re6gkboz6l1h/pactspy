AI technology automates and optimizes various software testing phases. Unlike traditional approaches involving manual script-writing and time-consuming execution, AI leverages advanced algorithms and machine learning to streamline the process. Here’s how AI uplifts testing procedures:

With breakthroughs like these, AI’s integration into continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) has established itself as a necessity rather than a luxury.
