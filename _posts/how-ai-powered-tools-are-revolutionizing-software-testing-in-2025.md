Overcoming Challenges and Limitations of AI in QA

Despite the remarkable benefits, AI in testing presents some limitations:

1. **Learning Curve:** Teams must adjust to new workflows, which can slow initial adoption.
2. **Model Bias:** AI outcomes depend on the quality and coverage of the training data, which can introduce errors.
3. **Cost Considerations:** Licensing fees and training investments can be significant for smaller organizations.
4. **Over-Reliance on Automation:** Certain creative or exploratory testing aspects still require human expertise.
5. **System Complexity Issues:** Complex systems may lead to AI-driven inconsistencies needing manual intervention.

Balancing AI integration with human oversight can mitigate these challenges, ensuring smooth deployment.
