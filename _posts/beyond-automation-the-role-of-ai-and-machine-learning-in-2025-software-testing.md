## Why Traditional Automation Falls Short

Automation tools like Selenium and Appium enhanced QA by streamlining repetitive tasks, but their limitations in dynamic, unpredictable scenarios call for intelligent testing solutions. Key drawbacks include scope restrictions, script maintenance requirements, and inefficiencies that hinder testing of complex, adaptive systems. AI introduces a paradigm shift, addressing these flaws through self-learning algorithms and predictive mechanisms.

## Game-Changer: Machine Learning in QA Processes

Machine learning stands out as a game-changer for QA, transforming testing workflows with:

1. **Self-Adaptive Test Scripts**: AI refines test scripts by autonomously reacting to application changes, minimizing manual interventions.
2. **Predictive Defect Analysis**: Leveraging usage patterns, AI predicts high-risk areas prone to defects earlier.
3. **Automated Exploratory Testing**: Uncover hidden system flaws with AI-driven exploratory testing techniques.
