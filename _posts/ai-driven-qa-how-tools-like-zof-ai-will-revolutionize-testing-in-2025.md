Integrating AI into QA requires resources and organizational change. Here’s the downside:

Users need training to master machine learning fundamentals and test automation frameworks. 

### 2. Deployment Costs
Initial setup for AI QA testing involves expenses for tools, resources, and training efforts. Long-term ROI, however, justifies the costs for many companies.

### 3. Reliance on Data Quality
AI depends on historical datasets, which may pose barriers for businesses without legacy data to train the AI system. Building large, clean datasets from scratch takes time.
