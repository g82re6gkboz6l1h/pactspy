Traditional testing methods, though effective, frequently face significant limitations in terms of time consumption and susceptibility to human error. ### Key Features of AI Testing Tools:

- **Predictive Analytics**: Flag potential vulnerabilities using historical data.
- **Streamlined Test Frameworks**: Automatically optimize workflows to save time.
