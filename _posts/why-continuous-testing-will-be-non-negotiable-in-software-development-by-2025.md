## Introduction to Continuous Testing in Agile and DevOps

Continuous testing integrates automated tests throughout the development lifecycle to deliver rapid feedback on risks tied to software releases. Unlike traditional testing that created bottlenecks, continuous testing allows agile and DevOps teams to release high-quality products efficiently, helping businesses unlock faster time-to-market and innovation.

## Key Drivers of Continuous Testing Adoption
- **Demand for Rapid Time-to-Market:** Continuous testing accelerates cycles for faster feature delivery.
- **Complex Software Infrastructure:** Microservices and containerized systems demand scalable validation.
- **Shift-Left & Shift-Right Testing:** Early bug detection and production validation become seamless.
- **AI and ML Advancements:** Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) automate redundant tasks and enhance test script maintenance.
- **Compliance Necessities:** Regulations like GDPR demand automated testing for adherence.
