Shifting from QA to Quality Engineering

The traditional quality assurance (QA) model is evolving into a more collaborative, integrated approach called quality engineering. Teams now embed quality into every phase of development, aiming to prevent defects at the source instead of addressing them post-deployment.

By 2025, quality engineering will emphasize:

- Shift-left testing methodologies, identifying problems earlier in the process.
- Dynamic testing frameworks powered by AI platforms like Zof AI.
- Continuous feedback loops to align application quality with user needs.

Organizations that adopt quality engineering, paired with tools like Zof AI, will experience faster development cycles, reduced costs, and a focus on customer satisfaction.
