## Why Performance Testing Matters for High-Traffic Applications
Performance testing evaluates how an application handles various workloads, ensuring optimal functioning even during traffic surges. Learn how this critical process prevents revenue loss, identifies bottlenecks, and prepares applications to scale seamlessly.

### Benefits of Performance Testing for High-Traffic Apps:
1. **Minimize Downtime:** Avoid crashes or slow speeds during peak periods to maintain user satisfaction and sales.
2. **Optimize Scalability:** Detect scalability issues before traffic increases.
3. **Pinpoint Inefficiencies:** Discover and resolve bottlenecks in servers, databases, and code for improved performance.
