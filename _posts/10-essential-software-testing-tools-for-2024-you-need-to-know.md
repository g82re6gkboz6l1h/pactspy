## Choosing the Right Testing Tools: Key Considerations

- **Simplicity**: User-friendly tools ensure accessibility for all skill levels.
- **Integration**: Compatibility with CI/CD tools like Jenkins or GitLab.
- **Automation**: Essential for repetitive and scalable tasks.
- **Scalability**: Supports expanding complexity and increased workloads.
- **Cross-Platform**: Compatibility with varied devices, OS, and browsers.
- **Cost vs. Features**: Balancing budget constraints with functionality.
