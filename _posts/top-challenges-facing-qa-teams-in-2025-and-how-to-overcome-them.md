Tackling Cybersecurity Challenges in QA

As cybersecurity concerns rise, QA must adapt to handle advanced threats. QA testing cannot solely focus on functionality but must incorporate robust vulnerability assessments integrating modern testing tools.

**Tips for Staying Secure in 2025:**
1. **Implement Penetration Testing**: Run automated hacking simulations to identify risks early.
2. **Adopt Shift-Left Testing**: Integrate security protocols earlier in development. Zof AI enhances early-stage vulnerability detection through CI/CD integration.
3. **Upskill QA Teams**: Train testers on the latest security trends and industry regulations.
4. **Regular Threat Modeling**: Anticipate vulnerabilities by adopting proactive modeling strategies.
