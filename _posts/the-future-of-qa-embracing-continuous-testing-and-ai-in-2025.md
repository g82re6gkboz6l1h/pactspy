### What is Continuous Testing, and Why is it Crucial in 2025?

Continuous testing involves running automated tests throughout the entire software development lifecycle, offering real-time insights into potential bugs and business risks. Traditional testing methods are struggling to keep up with the rapid iterations demanded by DevOps, Continuous Integration (CI), and Continuous Delivery (CD). 

**Benefits of Continuous Testing:**
- Faster feedback loops.
- Early risk detection.
- Real-time quality insights.
- Seamlessly integrated QA in all lifecycle stages.

To stay competitive, organizations are adopting intelligent automation and AI-driven tools, like Zof AI, to supercharge continuous testing.
