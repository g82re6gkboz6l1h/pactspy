## Preparing QA Teams for 2025: Key Challenges

1. **AI Integration**: Validate algorithms, test model outputs, and address AI model biases.
2. **Multi-Platform Testing**: Manage device diversity across IoT, AR/VR, and traditional platforms.
3. **Faster Turnarounds**: Cope with tighter deadlines using smarter automation and prioritization.
4. **Data Privacy & Security**: Ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR and HIPAA.
5. **Scalability**: Test systems handling millions of users and vast data volumes efficiently.
