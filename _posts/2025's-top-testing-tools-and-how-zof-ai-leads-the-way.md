## How Software Testing Tools Are Evolving

Software testing has come a long way—from manual approaches to AI-powered predictive systems. Here’s an overview of how the industry has reached this point:

1. **Manual Testing Dominance**: Tools like TestPad relied heavily on exploratory methods, leaving room for human error and slow execution.

2. **The Automation Revolution**: Selenium and JMeter introduced scripted automation, reducing redundancy yet requiring technical expertise.

3. **Continuous Testing in DevOps**: Jenkins and similar CI tools embedded testing workflows for faster bug resolution during development.

4. **AI Integration**: Platforms like Applitools and TestSigma leveraged artificial intelligence for smarter testing, paving the way for ongoing innovation.

Now, AI-driven platforms such as **Zof AI** are taking testing innovation to new levels, combining adaptability, precision, and seamless scalability.
