Human Oversight: Balancing Automation and Context

While AI-driven tools improve efficiency, human oversight ensures contextual and ethical considerations are met. Benefits of human oversight:
1. Understanding business-specific priorities.
2. Ethical decision-making in sensitive industries.
3. Interpretation of AI recommendations based on long-term goals.

To balance AI with manual input:
- Set restrictions on AI functionalities.
- Regularly audit AI decisions.
- Build team awareness of AI's potential and limitations.
