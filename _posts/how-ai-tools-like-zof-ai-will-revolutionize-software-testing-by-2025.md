## Why AI-Powered Software Testing Is Essential

Traditional software testing is a time-consuming task requiring manual test case design and execution. With increasingly complex software systems, these outdated methods often struggle to ensure test coverage or spot hidden bugs. AI-powered tools, such as Zof AI, address these limitations by automating testing, predicting system vulnerabilities, and continuously adapting to evolving codebases.

- **Defect Prediction**: Tools like Zof AI analyze data to foresee potential bugs before they manifest.
- **Continuous Testing**: Seamlessly integrates with DevOps pipelines to catch issues early in development.
