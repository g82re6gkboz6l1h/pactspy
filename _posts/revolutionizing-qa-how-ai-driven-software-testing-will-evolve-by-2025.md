## Revolutionizing QA: Major Trends to Watch

### 1. Self-Healing Test Automation
AI systems autonomously adapt to changes in applications, ensuring robust test coverage even after updates.

### 2. Hyper-Automation in Testing Processes
Combining AI, machine learning, and robotic process automation (RPA) will revolutionize QA workflows, making them faster and smarter by 2025.

### 3. Predictive Test Analytics
AI-powered predictive models pinpoint high-risk areas in software, helping developers address potential failures proactively.

### 4. Expansive Test Coverage Using AI
AI ensures better testing coverage, exploring deeper matrices of functionality and scenarios.

### 5. AI Support in Test-Driven Development (TDD)
AI-enabled test case generation accelerates TDD practices by integrating testing in early development stages.
