## Unleashing the Potential of AI in Software Testing

### From Manual to Predictive Testing

AI has reshaped the software testing lifecycle, moving beyond reactive and manual methods to implement predictive and proactive solutions. AI-powered tools analyze data, predict risks, and automate repetitive tasks—allowing teams to focus more on innovation and less on manual troubleshooting.

**Key advancements include:**
- Self-learning frameworks that auto-generate test cases based on defect trends.
- Natural language processing (NLP) for human-like test case creation.
- Visual testing powered by image-recognition AI for better UX evaluation.
- Automated test maintenance to adapt to evolving applications.
