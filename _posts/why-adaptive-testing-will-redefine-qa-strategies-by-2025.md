## What Is Adaptive Testing and Why It Matters

In an era of complex applications driven by AI, IoT, and cloud computing, **adaptive testing** emerges as a game-changer. It dynamically optimizes test cases based on system performance, user behavior, and code changes in real-time.

### Why Adaptive Testing is Essential by 2025:
- **Accelerated DevOps:** DevOps and agile methodologies demand faster releases—adaptive testing keeps pace.
- **Addressing Complexity:** Handles interactions with APIs, microservices, and devices efficiently.
- **Resource Optimization:** Focuses testing efforts on critical, high-risk areas.
- **Personalization Matters:** Covers unique, user-specific workflows that traditional testing misses.

Adaptive testing represents a smarter approach to the challenges of speed and complexity.
