## Challenges in Current QA Automation

QA automation has streamlined software testing but faces several limitations:

- **Test Maintenance Overload:** Traditional scripts require ongoing updates, draining productivity.
- **Limited Coverage:** Many tools struggle with testing complex workflows and dynamic systems.
- **Skill Barriers:** Scripting demands programming expertise, limiting accessibility for QA teams.
- **Static Models:** Testing scripts don’t adapt to rapid software changes.
- **Delayed Issue Detection:** Lack of predictive insights limits proactive error prevention.

These challenges highlight the need for intelligent and dynamic QA solutions—an area Zof AI is revolutionizing.
