## What is Predictive Analytics in Software Testing?

Predictive analytics employs data, machine learning, and statistical algorithms to predict potential future outcomes by analyzing historical data. In software testing, it anticipates bugs, optimizes testing efforts, and reduces risks. By examining past test outcomes, defect logs, and user behavior metrics, predictive analytics transitions testing from reactive to proactive — solving issues before they occur.

Tools like **Zof AI** utilize advanced machine learning models to prioritize testing, flag critical bottlenecks, and provide data-driven insights for streamlined workflows. By harnessing predictive analytics, development teams can minimize disruptions and ensure robust software performance.
