## The Evolution of AI in Software Testing

Software testing has traditionally been a reactive process, centered on identifying bugs post-development. Automated frameworks brought efficiency, yet relied on pre-programmed methods. AI disrupts this pattern, introducing predictive analytics, autonomous learning, and proactive testing approaches.

### How AI Revolutionizes QA:
- **Predictive QA:** Identifies vulnerabilities pre-emptively.
- **Self-Learning Models:** Adapt dynamically to application environments.
- **Scalable Automation:** Analyze vast datasets efficiently without human intervention.

AI enables tailored, proactive strategies for managing complex software ecosystems such as microservices and cloud-based platforms, redefining quality assurance methodologies.
