## 2. Benefits vs Challenges of AI Testing Automation

### Benefits:
- **Speed & Efficiency**: Automates repetitive tasks, reduces QA cycle time.
- **Cost Saving**: Minimizes long-term labor costs.
- **Pop-Up Bug Identification**: AI spots errors humans often overlook.
- **Intelligent Coverage**: Designs improved test cases autonomously.
- **Adaptability**: Self-healing systems adjust to code modifications.

### Challenges:
- **Skillset Training**: Requires upskilling QA professionals.
- **Data Quality**: Relies heavily on clean datasets.
- **Bias Risks**: Poorly trained AI can produce flawed results.
- **High Initial Investment**: Significant upfront technical and financial commitment.

Strategically navigating these challenges maximizes AI benefits in testing automation.
