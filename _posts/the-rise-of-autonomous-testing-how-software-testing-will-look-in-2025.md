## What is Autonomous Testing and Its Importance?

Autonomous testing is AI-driven software testing that reduces or eliminates the need for human intervention. Unlike traditional automation, autonomous systems learn application behavior dynamically, identify edge cases, and optimize test scenarios autonomously. This innovation meets the demands of modern, complex software systems spanning mobile, web, IoT, and cloud-native platforms.

- **Speed Up Development**: Agile and DevOps teams face tight deadlines. Autonomous testing eliminates bottlenecks by continuously executing tests.
- **Minimized Costs**: By catching bugs earlier, autonomous testing reduces the likelihood of costly patches post-release.
- **Accuracy**: AI-powered tools avoid human errors, ensure wider test coverage, and deliver precise reports.
