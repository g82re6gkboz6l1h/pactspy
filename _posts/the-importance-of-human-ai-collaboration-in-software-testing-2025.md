Striking the Balance Between Automation and Creativity

Maintaining harmony between AI-driven automation and human-centered testing ensures robust while empathetic software QA. Automation excels at scalability and precision, while testers tackle contextual, explorative scenarios. Examples:

1. **Banking Apps**: AI simulates attacks; humans validate intuitive workflows.
2. **Gaming**: AI tests performance; humans refine gameplay experiences.
3. **E-commerce**: AI flags bottlenecks; humans assess refund processing and user interaction.
