2. **Error Prediction and Prevention**: AI identifies bugs before they appear, improving system reliability.
3. **Continuous Testing and Adaptation**: AI tools adjust based on real-time data, optimizing regression testing.

Businesses like Zof AI are already demonstrating these advancements and pushing boundaries in QA innovation.
