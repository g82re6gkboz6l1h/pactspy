The Role of Blockchain & IoT in Software Validation

### Blockchain: Strengthening Transparency and Security
With blockchain technology creating secure, immutable ledgers, software testers will focus on:
- Real-time transaction validation and data integrity testing.
- Smart contract testing to avert vulnerabilities.
- Recording error-free audit trails for compliance.

### IoT: Tackling Device Complexity
IoT networks are projected to exceed billions of devices by 2025, demanding focused QA efforts to validate:
- Cross-device compatibility across varying environments.
- Performance under real-time network behaviors.
- Data privacy and security protocols.
