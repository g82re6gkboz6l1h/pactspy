## The Rapid Evolution of Software Systems

The rise of microservices, AI applications, IoT, and edge computing has made software systems more complex than ever. Key areas driving these changes include:

- **IoT & Hyper-Connectivity:** With over 75 billion IoT devices predicted by 2025, systems must integrate flawlessly. Testing protocols must adapt to diverse architectures and emerging protocols.

- **AI in Applications:** AI-driven systems require specialized testing strategies, such as reducing bias, increasing accuracy, and addressing edge cases.

- **Dynamic User Expectations:** Teams need to ensure that software can scale dynamically and meet user demands for personalized, real-time interaction.
