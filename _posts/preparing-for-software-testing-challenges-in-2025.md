Methodologies like Agile and DevOps demand rapid releases. By 2025, QA teams will need smart tools like **Zof AI** to deliver accurate, faster testing while avoiding bottlenecks.

### 3. Real-World Testing Requirements
Testing has evolved beyond lab simulations. Verifying performance across global devices, platforms, and environments will remain a key priority for QA teams.

### 4. Talent Shortage
QA specialists must master automation, AI, and machine learning to effectively navigate new tools and methods.
