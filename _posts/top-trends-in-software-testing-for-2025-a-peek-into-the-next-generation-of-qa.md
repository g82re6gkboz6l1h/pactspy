## Software Testing Trends to Watch in 2025

### AI-Powered Testing
Artificial Intelligence is the backbone of next-gen QA, enabling predictive analytics to tackle bugs before they occur. AI taps into automation, smart debugging, and preemptive measures that streamline testing processes.

### Hyperautomation
Hyperautomation integrates AI-ML into fully automated workflows, reducing effort, time, and costs while accelerating deployment. By leveraging hyperautomation, QA achieves unmatched efficiency.

### IoT & Edge Testing
With the rise of connected devices and real-time data, QA teams must address IoT application testing challenges such as cybersecurity vulnerabilities, low latency, and seamless device interactions.

### Blockchain Testing Frameworks
As blockchain grows, specialized testing solutions are crucial to ensure the reliability of decentralized platforms and applications.
