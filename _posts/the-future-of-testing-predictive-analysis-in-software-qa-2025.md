## What is Predictive Analysis in Software Testing?

Predictive analysis uses AI and machine learning to analyze historical testing data and identify potential risks before software deployment. Unlike traditional reactive approaches, predictive analysis equips QA teams with actionable foresight, ensuring smoother processes and fewer production issues. By applying predictive tools like Zof AI, developers can transform testing into a proactive strategy, predicting defect hotspots, optimizing test cases, and automating analysis.
