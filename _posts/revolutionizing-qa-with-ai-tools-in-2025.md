## Benefits and Challenges of Automated Testing

Modern QA strategies revolve around automation, offering unmatched scalability and efficiency. However, businesses also face challenges when integrating AI.

### Advantages of AI-based QA
- **Speed:** Faster execution of test cases, reducing deployment cycles.
- **Accuracy:** Elimination of human error with greater test coverage.
- **Cost-saving:** Reduced manual labor and quicker troubleshooting offset initial costs.
- **Scalability:** Seamlessly accommodates more complex systems.

### Existing Challenges
- High implementation and infrastructure costs.
- Reliance on diverse and extensive datasets for accurate results.
- Evolving test relevance in dynamic environments.
