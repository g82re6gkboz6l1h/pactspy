Traditional software testing methods such as manual testing and rule-based automation have faced scalability challenges and human limitations. By 2025, AI is enabling QA teams to stay ahead in the fast-paced development lifecycle.

1. **Automated Regression Testing**: AI eliminates time-consuming repetitive processes like regression testing and log analysis while generating smarter test cases.

2. **Smarter Bug Detection**: AI-powered tools use machine learning to spot defects with unmatched precision.

3. **Enhanced Coverage**: AI analyzes datasets to ensure exhaustive testing, including edge cases often missed manually.

4. **Optimized Test Prioritization**: Algorithms focus on high-risk segments of code, making QA efforts more strategic.

AI tools such as **Zof AI** represent the forefront of these advancements, making them indispensable for modern enterprises.
