## The Present: AI’s Role in Software Testing

AI is already reshaping software testing by automating repetitive tasks and enhancing accuracy. Machine learning algorithms are used to predict anomalies, optimize test case prioritization, and identify defects before they cause larger issues. 

AI-powered tools help QA teams pinpoint flaky tests, suggest fixes, and manage extensive test suites more efficiently. By leveraging historical data, these tools prioritize areas with higher defect probability, optimizing coverage while reducing redundancy.

Although promising, barriers such as limited expertise and lack of standardization hinder widespread AI adoption. Addressing these challenges will be vital for maximizing AI’s potential in quality assurance.
