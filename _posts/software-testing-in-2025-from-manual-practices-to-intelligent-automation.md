## Manual Testing Phases Out

### **Why Manual Testing Is Declining**
- Historical relevance in uncovering nuanced software defects.
- Limitations such as time consumption, variability, and scalability.
- Traditional testing struggles to keep up with modern agile development lifecycles.

### The Role of Automation
AI-powered tools streamline repetitive tasks such as regression testing, ensuring faster and consistent execution. Automated workflows deliver precision and scalability across diverse environments, making manual testing inefficient for today's high-speed development demands.
