## Key Selection Criteria for Automated Testing Tools

When choosing an automated testing tool, prioritize the following:

1. **Scalability:** Handle increased workloads as your application grows.
2. **AI Integration:** Essential for advanced test case generation and predictive debugging.
3. **Ease of Use:** Accessible to team members with varying technical expertise.
4. **Multi-Platform Support:** From APIs to web and mobile applications.
5. **CI/CD Integration:** Must align with Agile and DevOps workflows.
6. **Community and Updates:** Active community support and regular updates ensure longevity.
