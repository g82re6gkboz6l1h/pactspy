## Why Test Automation Is the Backbone of Modern Development

### 1. Accelerated Release Cycles
Continuous delivery dominates the software industry in 2025, pushing organizations into rapid, multiple daily deployments. Test automation ensures timely delivery without compromising quality.

### 2. Addressing Complex Applications
Applications intertwined with IoT, AI, and cloud-native systems require extensive validation, achievable only through automation.

### 3. Cost Savings and Efficiency Gains
Automated tests reduce repetitive manual efforts and streamline operations, saving organizations time and resources.
