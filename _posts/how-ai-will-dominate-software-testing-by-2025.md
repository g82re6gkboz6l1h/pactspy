## Why AI is the Future of Software Testing

As applications grow more complex, traditional testing struggles to meet increasing demands. AI bridges the gap through:
- Automating repetitive tasks such as test case creation, execution, and reporting.
- Enhancing test coverage by identifying critical edge cases using real-world usage data.
- Improving accuracy by detecting overlooked issues and analyzing massive datasets instantly.

AI ensures faster, higher-quality software releases with fewer errors and optimized workflows.
