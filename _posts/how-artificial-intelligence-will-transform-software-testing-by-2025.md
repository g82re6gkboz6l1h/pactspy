## A Brief History of AI in Testing

The journey of integrating AI into software testing spans several decades. In the 1990s, rule-based systems were used for testing but lacked flexibility. However, breakthroughs in machine learning (ML) and natural language processing (NLP) have dramatically enhanced AI's ability to execute dynamic testing. By 2025, advancements in big data, computational power, and AI frameworks will position AI-driven testing as a fundamental element of quality assurance (QA) strategies.
