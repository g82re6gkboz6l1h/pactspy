Gartner predicts hyper-automation will dominate tech innovations by 2025, enabling rapid scalability and error reduction.

In software development, hyper-automation optimizes testing processes, eliminating manual inefficiencies. Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) deliver self-learning systems that adapt to software evolutions for robust, scalable, and automated QA practices.
