## A Timeline of Test Automation Evolution

Since the late 20th century, test automation has progressed from rudimentary scripting and basic recordings to complex frameworks like Selenium and JUnit. These solutions simplified repetitive tasks but came with challenges, such as high maintenance needs and an inability to match the rapidly changing demands of modern software development. As machine learning found its way into test automation in the 2010s, it unlocked predictive capabilities, but adoption remained limited. Now, as AI-powered platforms like Zof AI enter the mainstream, the industry is on the brink of revolutionary change.
