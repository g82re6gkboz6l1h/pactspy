Intelligent test automation utilizes artificial intelligence and machine learning to revolutionize and optimize traditional software testing methods. Unlike conventional automation, intelligent systems are adaptive, predictive, and self-improving. Features like self-learning capabilities, predictive error detection, dynamic scalability, and cost efficiency make intelligent tools indispensable for managing modern software environments.

### Why Traditional Automation Is Becoming Outdated
While traditional tools like Selenium served well in the past, they fall short when tackling complex, dynamic applications.
