## What is AI in Software Testing?

AI in software testing utilizes machine learning and intelligent algorithms to automate and optimize tasks such as test case generation, defect identification, and predictive analytics. Unlike manual methods, which are time-consuming and error-prone, AI systems analyze vast volumes of data, adapt in real-time, and deliver superior results. For example, AI significantly enhances bug detection, ensuring no defect is overlooked and improving software reliability.

Additionally, AI empowers advanced testing dynamics like self-healing tests, risk-based prioritization, and automated regression testing, helping organizations keep up with rapidly changing and complex digital landscapes.
