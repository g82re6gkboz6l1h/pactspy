Heightened security testing will become mandatory as cyber threats grow and systems become interconnected.

## Key Technologies Revolutionizing Testing

1. **Cloud-Based QA Automation:** As businesses scale globally, cloud testing holds potential for simulating real-world environments while reducing infrastructure costs.

2. **IoT Testing at Scale:** Testing IoT devices becomes critical as wearables and smart appliances grow. Address hardware-software compatibility, security, and cross-platform performance.

3. **Blockchain QA Solutions:** Blockchain extends beyond cryptocurrencies; learn testing frameworks for cryptographic validation and peer transactions by 2025.
