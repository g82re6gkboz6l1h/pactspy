## What is Hyperautomation in QA?

Hyperautomation transcends conventional automation by embedding Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies into a unified testing ecosystem. These innovations ensure:

- **Interoperability** between platforms.
- **Scalability** for fast-paced development.
- **Proactive issue detection** with predictive analytics.
- **Optimized efficiency** for timely feedback loops.

By 2025, tech-savvy organizations will leverage hyperautomation frameworks to achieve seamless QA operations.
