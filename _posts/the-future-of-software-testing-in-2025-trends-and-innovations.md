Shift-Left and Shift-Right Testing Methodologies

Shift-left emphasizes early development testing to avoid compounding errors, while shift-right ensures post-deployment behavior aligns with performance goals. Combined methodologies extend test coverage across development and user environments. How AI tools enhance this transition is key to future industry success.
