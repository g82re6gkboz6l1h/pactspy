Challenges and Solutions for AI Adoption

Despite immense benefits, there are challenges to overcome when adopting AI in testing, such as:
- Data quality issues.
- The "black-box problem" (lack of AI decision explainability).
- High upfront costs for AI platforms.

### How to Address Challenges

1. **Invest in Data Quality**: Prioritize clean, validated datasets.
2. **Increase Transparency**: Use explainable AI (XAI).
3. **Cost-Efficient Models**: Opt for scalable AI tools like Zof AI with flexible pricing.
4. **Upskill Teams**: Train QA professionals to collaborate with AI platforms.
