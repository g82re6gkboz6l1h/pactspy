### Key Challenges in Agile Workflows
Agile sprints demand rapid coordination and adaptability:

1. **Time Constraints**: Short sprints make exhaustive traditional testing impractical.
2. **High Collaboration Needs**: Testers must integrate seamlessly with cross-functional teams.
3. **Changing Requirements**: Shifting user demands make static test plans obsolete.
4. **Automation Limitations**: Scaling test automation to match agile speed is tough.
5. **Quality in Small Increments**: Continuous delivery can lead to unresolved tech debt.
