## Biggest Software Testing Challenges in 2025

As the demand for high-quality software grows, QA teams will face increasing challenges, including:

1. **Shorter Delivery Cycles**: Rapid development frameworks like Agile leave little time for testing.
2. **Third-Party Dependencies**: Modern apps rely on APIs, increasing testing intricacy.
3. **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Ensuring flawless user experiences across devices and browsers.
4. **AI Complications**: Testing dynamic, ethical, and probabilistically behaving AI systems.
5. **Balancing Cost, Speed, and Scope**: Achieving end-to-end software coverage amidst tight budgets.
