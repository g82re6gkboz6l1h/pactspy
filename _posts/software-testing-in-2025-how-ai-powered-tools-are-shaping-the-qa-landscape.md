## Top Software Testing Trends for 2025

### 1. Automation at Scale
AI tools will auto-generate test cases based on application data, replacing time-intensive manual scripting.

### 2. Shift-Left Testing
QA embedded earlier in development pipelines ensures proactive defect management.

### 3. Predictive Quality Analytics
AI analyzes historical data to anticipate bugs and optimize testing focus.
