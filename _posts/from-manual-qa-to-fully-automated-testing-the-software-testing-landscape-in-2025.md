Benefits of Automation in Software Development

### Speed: Instantaneous Feedback Loops
Automation shrinks the feedback loop, providing real-time test results, actionable fixes, and predictive insights to boost development efficiency.

By relying on pre-configured scripts and AI-based learning, automated testing delivers more consistent results, even in edge cases.

### Scalability & Cost Effectiveness
