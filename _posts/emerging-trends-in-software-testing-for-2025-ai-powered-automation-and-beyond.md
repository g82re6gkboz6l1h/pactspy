## How AI Revolutionizes Testing Processes

By 2025, AI will redefine software testing with machine learning algorithms capable of adaptive workflows, defect prediction, and enhanced regression testing. Unlike static automation tools, AI processes massive datasets dynamically, generating automated test cases based on real-time updates. It enables faster error detection, identifies vulnerabilities, and enhances coverage—all with unmatched accuracy.
