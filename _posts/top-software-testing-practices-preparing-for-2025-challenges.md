### Key Software Testing Trends for 2025
- **Agile and DevOps**: Testing embedded at every stage for streamlined development.
- **AI and ML**: Predictive algorithms enhance test automation and defect tracking.
- **Shift-Left Testing**: Early defect detection lowers costs and improves efficiency.
- **User-Centric Design**: Focus on usability and accessibility testing.
- **Microservices Testing**: Adapting to complex, dynamic architectures.
