Software testing is entering a transformative phase as technologies like AI, machine learning, and hyperautomation impact the quality assurance (QA) industry. This article delves into the top software testing trends for 2025, including AI-driven tools, codeless test automation, and expanding testing to IoT, blockchain, and 5G applications. Discover actionable strategies for businesses and QA professionals to stay competitive and adapt to these changes, ensuring smarter, scalable, and efficient testing practices.
