Challenges in Adopting AI Models for QA

### 1. High Initial Costs
AI solutions require investment in tools and training.

### 2. Workforce Resistance
Teams need training to adapt to AI-enhanced QA workflows.

### 3. Data Quality Dependence
AI’s accuracy hinges on clean, unbiased data inputs.

### 4. Bias Risks
Incomplete training data creates skewed results.

### 5. Security Concerns
Sensitive application data exposure remains a pivotal challenge.

### 6. Evolving QA Roles
Automation alters QA responsibilities, emphasizing human oversight.
