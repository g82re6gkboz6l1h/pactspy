Challenges of AI Integration in QA Processes

While promising, AI-driven testing also has hurdles to overcome:

### 1. **Dependence on Data**
AI’s effectiveness hinges on the quality and diversity of its training data.

### 2. **Interpreting Results**
Complex AI-driven algorithms require experienced personnel to decipher insights.

### 3. **Initial Investment Costs**
AI implementations demand upfront capital, potentially challenging small businesses.

### 4. **Human-AI Balance**
Complete reliance on AI risks missing creative, intuitive problem-solving abilities unique to human testers.
