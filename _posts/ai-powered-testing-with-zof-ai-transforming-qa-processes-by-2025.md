Challenges & Opportunities in Adopting AI in QA

### Challenges in AI-Driven Testing:
- **Dependency on Data:** Requires quality historical data for optimal functionality.
- **AI Biases:** Risks from skewed training data.
- **Steep Learning Curve:** New users may initially struggle with AI adoption.
- **Manual Testing Reliance:** Skepticism surrounding full automation.

### Opportunities Offered:
- **Broader Test Coverage:** Detailed insights into all layers of applications.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Streamlined, scalable testing at minimal expenses.
- **Proactive Risk Assessment:** Identifying weak links early on.
- **Improved Collaboration:** Strengthening QA teamwork with tech and non-tech alignment.

Moving past challenges will enable organizations to reap the full potential of AI-powered testing tools.
