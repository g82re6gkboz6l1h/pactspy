## A Glimpse into the AI-Driven Future of QA

### Role Shifts in QA:
1. **Execution to Oversight**: Strings of repetitive tasks like regression testing will be handed off to AI. Testers will focus on supervising systems to root out bias and errors.
2. **Strategic Leadership**: QA professionals will devise challenging test cases AI might overlook, elevating their roles from executioners to architects.
3. **Data Analysis Proficiency**: Insights generated by AI need validation and interpretation—testers will master data trends analysis.
