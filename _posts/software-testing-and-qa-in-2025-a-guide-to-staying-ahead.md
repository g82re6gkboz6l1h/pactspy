Testing Microservices with Advanced Strategies

Microservices introduce unique challenges, requiring comprehensive testing strategies like contract, integration, and end-to-end tests. Platforms like Kubernetes and Docker facilitate containerized testing environments, ensuring early detection of integration issues. AI-powered tools dynamically map dependencies while chaos engineering prepares systems for failure. Mastering microservices testing will be crucial for QA engineering in 2025.
