Automated testing leverages tools and scripts to perform pre-designed test cases on software systems with minimal manual input. This approach ensures accuracy, scalability, and rapid feedback cycles that are essential in agile environments.

Key benefits of automated testing include:
- Faster regression and repetitive testing processes.
- Enhanced test coverage across diverse environments.
- Consistent, human-error-free results.
