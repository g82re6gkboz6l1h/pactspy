The software testing industry is undergoing rapid evolution due to advancements in technologies like AI, machine learning, IoT, cloud computing, and automation. As companies increasingly integrate these innovations into their products and workflows, the need for skilled software testers who can keep up with these changes is growing. To excel in software testing by 2025, you must anticipate trends, acquire in-demand skills, and leverage cutting-edge tools.
